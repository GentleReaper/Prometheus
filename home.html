Welcome to Phoenix Donation

Phoenix Donation is a platform dedicated to connecting organ donors with recipients in need. Together, we can save lives and give hope to families across Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

To make organ donation accessible, transparent, and secure. We believe every person deserves a second chance at life, and through your generosity, we can make that possible.
